About Bettiah Police

West Champaran (Bettiah) District Police is a part of Champaran Police range. It touches Nepal in North and U.P. in west. It is located 225 kilometres northwest of Patna, in the state of Bihar, having naturally enriched spot like bhikhnathodi in gaunaha block and udaypur wild life sanctuary.There are three police sub-divisions in Bettiah sadar-1, sadar-2 and narkatiyaganj and having four circles Sadar, Yogapatti, Mainatand and Shikarpur. it has totally 35 police stations (including dedicated Police Station for Traffic, Cyber, Mahila and SC/ST ) which are actively engaged in ensuring security, law and order for 24x7 services and dedicated to public safety and maintaining order, fostering community co-operation, being taken immense efforts towards handling crime and striving to enhance the quality of life under the leadership of the Superintendent of Police, Bettiah
